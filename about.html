Hi, I’m Hasina

I created this baking space to share the joy, comfort, and creativity I find in making sweet treats. Baking has always been a way for me to relax, express myself, and bring happiness to the people I love.

My Baking Story

I started baking as a fun hobby, experimenting with simple recipes and slowly learning how to make them beautiful. Over time, I fell in love with the process — the soft colors, the cozy atmosphere, and the feeling of creating something warm and delicious from scratch.

Things I Love to Bake

  • Soft, fluffy cupcakes
  • Warm cookies with gooey centers
  • Aesthetic cakes with pastel colors
  • Simple, cozy treats for everyday moments
