My Favorite Bakes

Soft, cozy treats that bring warmth and sweetness to any day.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ cup softened butter
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tbsp vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients.
  3. Add butter, eggs, milk, and vanilla.
  4. Mix until smooth.
  5. Fill cupcake liners ¾ full.
  6. Bake 18–20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Cream butter and sugars.
  2. Add eggs.
  3. Mix in dry ingredients.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop onto baking sheet.
  6. Bake at 350°F for 10–12 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 box white cake mix
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 packet strawberry Jell‑O
  • Fresh strawberries (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients.
  2. Pour into a greased pan.
  3. Bake at 350°F for 25–30 minutes.
  4. Top with whipped cream + strawberries.
